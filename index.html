About the band

Cairokee is an influential Egyptian rock band formed in Cairo in 2003. Known for blending rock, reggae, and blues with Arabic music elements, their music is characterized by socially relevant lyrics and energetic performances. Rising to fame with their debut album "Matloob Zaeem" in 2009, the band's songs often address societal issues and politics, becoming anthems during the Egyptian Revolution in 2011.
